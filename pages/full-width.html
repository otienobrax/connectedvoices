Umoja Camp Meeting 2021

I will go with the everlasting Gospel


It’s camp meeting season once again. The church looks forward to the support of all its members as we prepare to come together to commemorate a memorable week-long spiritual event in the midst of the Covid-19 pandemic


This year, we thank God for enabling us to hold two Camp meeting events. We begin with the Children's Ministries Camp Meeting running from July 17th -24th. Click here to follow our Online Children's program

LIVE STREAM


Our camp meeting expense of 3.4 Million Kshs calls for our sacrificial giving and the blessing of the Holy assembly will call for our devout attendance & earnest prayers..

Remarks

  • From Pst. Peter Kebaso kebaso - District Pastor

    Cordial greetings in Jesus name. I wish to kindly welcome you all to the first ever children's ministries camp meeting in SDA church Umoja. Proverbs 22:6 , "Train up your children in the way they should and when they grow of age, they will not depart from it."The best way to invest in our children is to give them o spiritual foundation. The ways and means you will invest in your child today will go a long way to shape their future lives. The attitudes we have towards our children will determine how far they will go in respect to all balances of life. "As the twig bends so is the tree".Let's all wake up and invest in our children for this life and the life to come. Welcome all.

  • From Eld. Evans Tongi - First Elder

    My Dear Brethren! Greetings in the name of our Lord and savior Jesus Christ I take this opportunity to thank our Almighty God for having kept us safe through the year despite the challenges we face. On behalf of the Church pastor, Elders, Umoja church leaders and my own behalf, I welcome all our children, their friends and neighbors to our inaugural children’s camp meeting which is scheduled for 18th July to 24th July, 2021. The theme of the camp meeting is “I Will Go With The Everlasting Gospel”. Let us therefore empower our children so that the shall be bearers of the good news of eternal life wherever they are and wherever they go. To our guests and teachers please feel welcomed and at Jesus’ feet and may our God Lord use you mightily to His glory and honor. we are all excited to have this meeting and I believe the experience will be enormous and satisfying especially to our children. God bless you all

  • From Eld. Gabriel Okuthe - Camp Chairman

    I want to take this opportunity to thank our God for keeping us safe to this important occasion. This being the first Children’s Camp Meeting in our church, the event is one of it's kind and historical in our church. It is therefore important that all children find time and attend the campmeeting. The enemies of truth are many, and because our numbers are few, we should present as strong a front as possible. Individually, children need the benefits of the campmeeting, and God calls upon them to be position one in the ranks of truth. If the children of Israel needed the benefit of these holy convocations during their time, how much more do we need them in these last days of peril and conflict? And if the people of the world then needed the light which God has committed to his church, how much more do they need it now? This is your time to come up and reason with God. I desire you be acquainted with His teachings and work. Welcome to this gracious occasion.

  • From Sis. Naomi Moraa - Director, Children’s ministries

    Welcome to the Seventh-day Adventist Church, Umoja inaugural Children's Camp meeting. This is the first time we are hosting a dedicated camp meeting for our children and the Children’s Ministries Department have a pleasure to coordinate this occasion. Let us prepare to come together to commemorate this week-long spiritual event for our children in the midst of the Covid-19 pandemic. The theme "I will go with the everlasting gospel" is timely for our children, as they will understand the urgency of spreading the gospel to all the world in this generation. Do join us and share in the camp meeting blessing.
